My favorite game is Overwatch. I started playing in OW2, sharing a friend's account since I didn't know how much I was going to be playing. Combined with my new personal account, I have 950+ hours on the game and am still hard stuck in Bronze. I guess that's what you get for it being your first FPS game. Most of the time I play either support or DPS, but I can flex to tank if needed. I like to play Baptiste, Moira, Kiriko, and have recently been dabbling in some Mercy gameplay. For DPS, I like Sombra, Mei, Pharrah, and have been trying to get better at Widowmaker. I'm not the best at tank, but I can play D.va, Orisa, and occasionally Zarya.
I also play modded Minecraft a lot. I have a server with some friends where we play a lot of the Create mod. The pack we're playing on also has a lot of Farmer's Delight type of mods. Although I'm a wuss about going into the Nether, I'm trying to be stronger. I enjoy the engineering aspect of Create and have been trying to build a lot of good looking buildings on the server.
As you can see by my page, I like the 90s aesthetic a lot. Although I don't remember much of the 90s, being a late 90s baby myself, I've always been drawn to the style. I like the bright colors and the blocky shapes. I also enjoy Mtn Dew Baja Blast.
